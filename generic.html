When the seagulls...

'When the seagulls
follow the trawler
it is because they think sardines will be thrown into the sea' - Eric Cantona

When I was in year 6, my school took me and my classmates on my first ever holiday. It was a residential at one of Whitby's many accomodation and if I recall correctly it was in an nunnery, it even could've been in the Hostel.

When I got my camera I wondered for a good few weeks what I could do to enhance my photographic skillset whilst ticking off of one on my bucket list. It seemed Whitby was the answer, so after booking a coach that is what I did exactly.

Strolling along the pier really made a great space for photos like these, I really enjoyed the colour depth which I could capture here.

The blend of colours on this photo really compliment the scenery here too. The men on the boat also bring an element of everyday life to Whitby and this inspired me greatly.

The contrast on the red hut placed on the pier is what I believe to make this photo interesting, I later tried to go below the hut however the photos I took there weren't to my satisfaction due to user error.

I took a walk along the coast and gained a different perspective to aviod taking repetitive photos.

This is one of my favourites which I feel is overshined by the rest.

This one was actually taken in the morning, believe it or not
